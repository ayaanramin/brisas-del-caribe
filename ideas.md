# Brisas del Caribe - Design Brainstorm

## Design Approach Selected: Warm Caribbean Authenticity with Modern Sophistication

**Design Movement:** Contemporary Caribbean Hospitality meets Urban Bronx Authenticity

**Core Principles:**
1. **Warmth & Approachability** - Earthy, sun-baked tones that evoke Caribbean beaches and Dominican heritage without feeling touristy
2. **Sales-Driven Clarity** - Every section has a clear conversion path (Order Now, Call, Catering Inquiry) with prominent CTAs
3. **Local Pride & Credibility** - Authentic storytelling about family ownership, fresh ingredients, and community roots in Castle Hill
4. **Modern Minimalism with Soul** - Clean layouts that don't feel sterile; breathing room paired with warmth

**Color Philosophy:**
- **Primary Warm Tones:** Deep terracotta (#C85A3A), warm sand (#E8D4B8), rich chocolate (#3D2817)
- **Accent Colors:** Vibrant tropical green (#2D7A5C for CTAs), sunset orange (#F08D4D for highlights)
- **Neutrals:** Off-white cream (#F9F7F4), warm gray (#8B7D6B)
- **Emotional Intent:** Evokes sun-warmed wood, fresh plantains, ocean breezes, and family gatherings around food

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Asymmetric hero with large appetizing food image on right, text/CTAs on left
- Diagonal section dividers (subtle SVG waves) to break monotony
- Staggered featured dishes grid (alternating left-right image placement)
- Sticky navigation that becomes transparent over hero, solidifies on scroll
- Mobile-first with full-width sections that stack elegantly

**Signature Elements:**
1. **Tropical Wave Dividers** - Subtle SVG wave patterns between sections (Caribbean ocean reference)
2. **Warm Gradient Overlays** - Soft terracotta-to-transparent gradients over food images
3. **Handwritten Accent Font** - Script font for taglines and section headers (family-owned authenticity)

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Smooth scroll-triggered animations (images fade in, text slides up)
- Hover effects on menu items show price and description
- CTAs have warm glow/shadow on hover
- Mobile: Hamburger menu slides from left with warm background
- Forms have warm focus states (terracotta border, subtle glow)

**Animation Guidelines:**
- Entrance animations: Fade + slide-up (200ms, ease-out) for content blocks
- Hover: Subtle scale (1.02x) and color shift on menu items
- Scroll animations: Parallax on hero image (slower scroll rate)
- Transitions: All 300ms cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1) for smoothness
- Avoid: Bouncy animations, excessive motion (keep it sophisticated)

**Typography System:**
- **Display Font:** Playfair Display (serif, bold) - Restaurant name, section headers, testimonials
- **Body Font:** Inter (sans-serif, 400/500) - Menu descriptions, body text, CTAs
- **Accent Font:** Caveat (handwritten) - Tagline, special labels, "Daily Specials"
- **Hierarchy:** H1 48px/bold (hero), H2 36px/bold (sections), H3 24px/medium (subsections), Body 16px/400

**Design Decisions:**
- No corporate blue; all warm earth tones
- Food imagery is hero (large, appetizing, professional)
- Social proof (testimonials) placed early to build trust
- Catering & ordering CTAs repeated throughout (sticky nav, hero, menu, footer)
- Map & hours visible on every section (local credibility)
- Mobile contact button (click-to-call) always accessible
